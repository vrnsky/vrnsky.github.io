### Work Clock Sentry: A Game for Developers Who Just Want to Code
Guard your focus time!

Ever been in the zone, crushing a complex algorithm, when suddenly "Got 5 min for a quick sync?" pops up? Work Clock Sentry turns that universal developer frustration into an arcade game.

The Concept

You're a developer at your desk trying to write code. Meeting invites rain down from above — dodge them while collecting commits for points. The longer you survive, the more meetings pile on.

Features

- 5 meeting types to avoid: Quick syncs, code reviews, standups, all-hands, and 1:1s
- Power-ups: DND Mode (invincibility), Speed Boost, and Commit Magnet
- Dual currency system: Earn Coffee Tokens by playing, purchase Premium Beans to unlock exclusive items
- Mobile-friendly: Play on desktop or tap controls on mobile
